Mission Statement:

Chicanx Caucus seeks to create an environment of support within the Columbia University Community for its membership. Through said support, the Caucus aims to help members achieve their educational goals, increase their cultural consciousness and political awareness and promote involvement in the Mexican, Mexican-American, Chicanx, and Latinx community.

The goals of this organization in achieving this mission statement shall be:

- To inform Chicanxs about higher education opportunities by offering objective information and sharing our personal experiences.

- To initiate and participate in activities that foster inter-group relations, interaction among the Chicanx Caucus, the Columbia Community, and the Chicanx, Mexican, Mexican-American, Latinx community of New York. This includes but is not limited to: educational opportunities, cultural experiences, political involvement, and gender and sexuality issues.

- To contribute through our active participation to the general well-being of Latinxs, and towards the proper resolution of the problems facing these analogous peoples.
